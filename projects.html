Personal Website

| HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Web development has always been a facet of technology that has piqued my interest. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I decided to focus on developing skills that would allow me to build and design my own personal website, through which I could showcase my extracurriculars, work experience, and side projects.

As such, I began researching front-end development and subsequently came across an online course hosted by Code with Mosh that could teach me how to build my own website on the IDE Visual Studio Code, utilizing HTML, CSS, and some JavaScript. Ultimately, I was educated on web development principles, HTML/CSS basics, layout, typography, images, forms, transitions, animations, etc.

The website you are currently viewing is the fruit of my labour! Of course, it is a work in progress, and I will be gradually adding new features and updating information as time goes on. Throughout the course of my computer science degree, this is one of the most rewarding projects I have had the pleasure of working on.
